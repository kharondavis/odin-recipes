Empanadas

Description

The Latin food item that is easy and fun to make.

Empanadas can have a variety of fillings for all different tastes and diet types! This recipe calls for Beef Empanadas. All options are optional of course.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Water
  • Unsalted butter
  • Ground beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Mexican spices
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, salt, butter, egg, and ice water in a food processor.
  2. Knead it for 5 minutes until it is smooth. Shape into 2 equal-sized balls, tightly cover each in plastic wrap, and refrigerate.
  3. Saute the tomato and onion in a large skillet with olive oil for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the garlic and cook for 30 more seconds.
  5. Add the ground beef and seasonings and cook for 6-8 minutes.
  6. Add the potato, carrot, and water.
  7. Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover, and cook for 10 more minutes until the veggies are soft and tender.
  8. Stir in peas and set aside.
  9. Take the dough out of the refrigerator and divide it into 18 equal pieces, then roll each piece into a ball.
  10. Roll each ball evenly into a 6½ to 7-inch circle with a rolling pin.
  11. Fill the empanadas with 3-4 tablespoons of filling to one side of the empanada dough.
  12. Top with a small pinch of shredded cheese.
  13. Fold the dough in half over the filling.
  14. Seal the empanada by pressing the edges together with the tines of a fork.
  15. Refrigerate for 15 minutes.
  16. Bake at 400°F for 25-30 min or until golden brown.
  17. Let them cool for 10 minutes before serving to help the filling set.
  18. Enjoy!
